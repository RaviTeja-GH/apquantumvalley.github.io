What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of a computer system to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include understanding language, recognizing images, decision making and problem solving.

Real Life Examples

  • Google Maps predicting fastest route
  • YouTube video recommendations
  • Face unlock in smartphones
  • Chatbots in customer support
  • Email spam filtering

AI vs Machine Learning

Artificial Intelligence is the overall concept of machines acting smart. Machine Learning is a subset of AI where systems learn automatically from data without being explicitly programmed.

Types of AI

  • Narrow AI: Performs a single specific task (Face unlock, spam filters, recommendation systems)
  • Generative AI: Creates new content like text, images and code (ChatGPT, Gemini, GitHub Copilot, Grok)
  • General AI: Human-level intelligence (still under research)

Why AI is Important in Industry

  • Automation of repetitive work
  • Fraud detection in banking
  • Customer support chatbots
  • Predictive maintenance in companies
